Eating & Drinking:

• Avoid heavy meals close to bedtime.

• Stop drinking beverages with caffeine at least 4-6 hours before bedtime.

• Avoid nicotine before bedtime or when you wake up at night.

• Avoid alcohol just before bedtime. Alcohol can cause you to wake up during the night even though it might have helped you fall asleep.

Relaxing Before Bedtime:

• Don't exercise before bedtime.

• Only do activities that are calm and relaxing.

• Take a warm bath or shower.

• Use relaxation techniques like deep breething or imagery.

• Listen to sound that relaxes you (white noise, calm music, guided meditation, etc).

• Don't "make an effort" to fall asleep.

Sleep Behaviour:

• Avoid reading, eating, or watching TV before bedtime.

• If unable to fall asleep within 20 minutes, get out of bed and engage in a quiet activity. Return to bed when sleepy.

• Go to bed only when sleepy.

• Use the bed and bedroom only for sleep.

Sleeping Environment:

• Keep the room a constant, cool tempreature throughout the night.

• Keep the room dark.

• Remove, cover, or replace brightly lit clocks and other electronic devices.

• Play constant low level sound, like calm music or calm white noise, throughout the night.

Timing:

• Avoid napping during the day.

• Go to bed at the same time each day.

• Get up at the same time every day no matter how long you slept.
